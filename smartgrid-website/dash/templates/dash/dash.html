The SmartGrid Command and Control Center can compute a handful of statistics from it's database of power consumption readings. The current computable statistics are mean, standard deviation, minimum, maximum, and range. In addition, you can compute these statistics on either past consumption or temperature readings. The command and Control Center also has the capability of uploading data. To use either of these features just click on the corresponding button!
